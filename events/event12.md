### About This Event

Ready to move past basic prompting and build AI that actually does things?

Welcome to **Agent Dev-Sprint**—a high-velocity, hands-on one-day event designed for developers, engineers, and AI builders to master autonomous agent architectures and put their code to the test in a live, gamified arena.

Whether you are looking to orchestrate LLM inference, implement advanced tool-use, or figure out how to keep agents from looping endlessly, this sprint bridges the gap between raw theory and production-ready execution.

### 🏎️ The Event Flow

We move fast. The day is structured into high-impact "Laps" to take you from a blank IDE to a competing agent by sunset.

**Lap 1: The Warm-Up (Technical Deep Dives)**  
Learn the core pillars of agent architecture—Perception, Reasoning (ReAct/Chain-of-Thought), and Tool-Calling.

**Lap 2: The Straightaway (Hacking Phase 1)**  
Set up your environment using our starter kits, build your core logic, and test local execution loops.

**The Pit Stop (Lunch & Mentorship)**  
Refuel, network, and unblock your code with hands-on help from our expert mentors.

**Lap 3: The Final Corner (Optimization)**  
Fine-tune your prompts, integrate custom tools, and run sandbox simulations to prepare for the tournament.

**The Checkered Flag (The Arena Showdown)**  
Deploy your agent endpoint to the live leaderboard and watch them battle it out head-to-head in a gamified environment.
