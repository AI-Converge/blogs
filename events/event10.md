### About This Event

Most AI demos don't survive contact with production. This event is about fixing that. 

The **Agentic Showdown** is a full-day, hands-on meetup for AI engineers, solution architects, and developers who are done with the hype and ready to build autonomous systems that actually work. No fluff, no slide decks—just real architectures, deep engineering war stories, and live hacking.

### Agenda

* **10:00 AM - 10:30 AM** - Arrival, Security Check-in & Coffee Briefing
* **10:30 AM - 11:30 AM** - Session 1: AI Agent Evals & LLM-as-Judge Frameworks
* **11:30 AM - 12:30 PM** - Session 2: Technical Deep Dive: Production Agents at Scale
* **12:30 PM - 1:15 PM** - Panel Discussion: Agents in the Wild (War Stories from the Field)
* **1:15 PM - 2:00 PM** - Networking Lunch & Team Formations
* **2:00 PM - 4:00 PM** - The Jam: 2-Hour Mini-Hackathon
* **4:00 PM - 4:45 PM** - Team Demos & Judging Panel
* **4:45 PM - 5:00 PM** - Award Ceremony, $500 Prize Distribution & Wrap-up

### 📊 Key Topics

**AI Agent Evals & Benchmarking:**
* Setting up reliable verification pipelines beyond generic accuracy metrics
* Implementing LLM-as-Judge patterns, execution guardrails, and regression testing across model upgrades

**Production Agents at Scale:**
* Managing high concurrency, strict state persistence, state checkpointing, and Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) architectures
* Breaking down the real infrastructure costs and latency bottlenecks of unconstrained autonomy

**The Jam (Mini-Hackathon):**
* Assembling into teams of 4–5 to tackle an unannounced, production-grade problem statement under a tight 2-hour deadline
* Building, debugging, and deploying a functional autonomous agent with direct mentor guidance
