### About This Event

The novelty of autonomous agents is wearing off, and the reality of production reliability is setting in. Building an agent is easy; "harnessing" it—ensuring it follows strict guardrails, utilizes context efficiently, and remains completely observable—is the real challenge. 

An LLM on its own is just a brain in a jar. Without a robust harness acting as its hands, eyes, memory, and safety boundaries, it cannot execute reliably. At this edition of **AI Converge**, we are moving entirely beyond the hype to discuss the structural engineering rigor required to build **Agent Harnesses**. We'll explore the sandboxed infrastructure that keeps agents on track and the evaluation frameworks that prove they actually work.
